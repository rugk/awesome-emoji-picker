This (Firefox) add-on (WebExtension) gives you a nice emoji picker that you can use to find and select emojis. It will be copied to your clipboard, when you click on it. It uses up-to-date Emojis and let's you access your Emojis in different styles you know from different devices and applications. It is based on the awesome emoji-mart.
